Intro to a Great Sport

Despite being one of the oldest current olympic competitions, wrestling is still a foreign entity to countless people throughout the world. If you're unfamiliar with the sport, these are the three different predominant styles of wrestling:

  • Freestyle
  • Greco-Roman
  • Folkstyle

Freestyle and Greco-Roman are the styles competed at the international level including the olympics, while folkstyle is almost solely competed in the United States. Here is an info table introducing you to some of the most elite American wrestlers of all time:
